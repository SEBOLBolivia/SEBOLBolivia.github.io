Past Conferences

Since 2009, SEBOL organizes an annual conference with the objective to bring together local and international researchers to encourage the free exchange of ideas and cuting edge research in all fields related to economics. Our annual conference is co-organized with the Institute for Advanced Studies in Development (INESAD) and the Bolivian Academy of Economic Sciences (ABCE). Each year the conference is hosted by the best universities in the country.
